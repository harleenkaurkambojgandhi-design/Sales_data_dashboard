📊 Sales Dashboard of Year 2024–2025

📦 Top Categories by Quantity
Monitor leads with 1,843 units, followed by Mouse (1,496) and CPU (1,450). Printers and NICs are the least sold categories (~357–359 units).

🏷️ Brand Split (Sales %)

Intel dominates at 18.89%, followed by Samsung (16%), Dell (14.34%), Nvidia (13.21%), and Western Digital (8.11%).
🗺️ State Performance
Maharashtra is the highest-grossing state by far, followed by Gujarat and Uttar Pradesh. Sales taper significantly beyond the top 5 states.
